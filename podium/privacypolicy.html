Privacy Policy

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in Podium.

We Collect No Personal Information Using Our Application

We do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data recorded in Podium.

Individual settings relating to the Podium app are not personal and are stored only on your device. We don't process that information at all and have no access to it.

To help us better understand how people use the Podium app and to continue improving upon it, we use analytics information. These services provide us with some generic usage, transaction, and other statistics that are completely anonymous and cannot personally identify you in any way.
